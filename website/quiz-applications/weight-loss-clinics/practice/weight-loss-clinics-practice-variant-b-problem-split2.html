Recognize These Frustrations in Your Clinic?

If you're nodding along to any of these, you're certainly not alone. These are common symptoms of underlying operational gaps:

  • "Program Leakage": Patients start strong but lose momentum or fall off track due to inconsistent follow-up or support systems.
